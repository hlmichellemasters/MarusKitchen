Sauteed Mushroom Cauliflower Rice with Kale and Hummus

A simple, creative meal made super quickly

Ingredients:

  • mushrooms
  • kale
  • cauliflower rice
  • garlic
  • oil
  • hummus
  1. Preheat a skillet with garlic and oil to medium to medium-high.
  2. Drop the mushrooms on the skillet and let cook down
  3. Microwave some cauliflower rice with a sprinkle of water for around 3-4 minutes.
  4. While mushrooms are cooking, chop up some kale
  5. Check your mushrooms(should be done in about 10 minutes.)
  6. Mix up your cauliflower rice with the hummus and kale.
  7. Once the mushrooms are cooked to your preference enjoy your meal in under 15 minutes!

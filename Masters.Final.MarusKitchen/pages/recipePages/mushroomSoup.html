Mushroom, Spinach, and Potato soup

Besides the chopping, this soup requires almost no watching over, meaning you can get your homework done or go for a walk while it cooks!

Ingredients:

  • mushrooms
  • potatoes
  • spinach
  • salt and pepper
  • mixed spices
  • optional: veggie broth or mushroom broth
  1. Prepare the mushrooms. Mine were the gourmet dried mushrooms that required soaking, but you could also use fresh for this!
  2. Chop up the potatoes.
  3. Put everything except the spinach into the Instant Pot with enough broth or water to cover
  4. Cook on "Soup" setting for 15 minutes.
  5. Once its done, top with spinach and mix in to lightly steam
  6. Enjoy your souper easy soup!

Blackened Salmon, Cauliflower Rice and a Cruciferous and Avocado Salad

This is a recipe that can be made super fast! But still creates the feel of a fancy dinner. Its fast because you can buy the ingredients almost already prepped for you at stores (like Trader Joe's)

Ingredients:

  • salmon fillet
  • avocado
  • cauliflower rice
  • garlic
  • oil
  • mixed salad veggies (like brussel sprouts, cabbages, kale or other greens)
  1. Preheat a skillet with garlic and oil to medium to medium-high.
  2. Drop the salmon fillet skin-side up on the skillet and place a lid on top.
  3. Microwave some cauliflower rice with a sprinkle of water for around 3-4 minutes.
  4. While salmon is still cooking, open a bag of mixed cruciferous (brussel sprouts, cabbages, and kale) and toss with your favorite dressing.
  5. Check your salmon (should be done in about 7-10 minutes.)
  6. Chop up your avocado and dress your salad.
  7. Enjoy your fancy meal made in under 15 minutes!

Squash, Walnut, and Greens Meal Prep Bowls

The rice and squash will take a little longer to make, but you can make enough meals to eat all week!

Ingredients:

  • delicata squash
  • rice
  • walnuts
  • mixed greens
  • peanut sauce
  • mixed veggies (like cabbage and carrots)
  1. Cut the squash in half , scoop out the seeds, and cook in the instant pot on high for 15 minutes
  2. Start your rice cooker with a few cups of rice with water and seasoning.
  3. Pour sauce and crunchy veggies on the bottom of th mason jar.
  4. Once the squash and rice is donelayer them into the jar.
  5. Next, top with greens and walnuts
  6. Enjoy your meals prepped for the whole week!

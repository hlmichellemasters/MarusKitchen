Cheezy Beans and Rice Meal Prep

Very easy meal prep, and also inexpensive!

Ingredients:

  • canned (black) beans
  • rice
  • cheez sauce
  • mixed greens
  • carrots (or any other preferred veg, like olives or cabbage
  1. Start the rice in a rice cooker
  2. Heat up your favorite canned beans (microwave).
  3. Pour beans and crunchy veggies, like carrots on the bottom of th mason jar.
  4. Once the rice is donelayer with the cheez sauce into the jar.
  5. Next, top with greens
  6. Enjoy your super easy meals prepped for the whole week!

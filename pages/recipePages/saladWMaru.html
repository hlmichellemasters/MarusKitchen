Fast Goddess Salad

Super fast salad for throwing together when you are too busy to cook at all

Ingredients:

  • mushrooms
  • guacamole / avocado
  • red cabbage
  • arugula
  • micro greens
  • goddess dressing, or some other tahini-based dressing
  1. Just layer all the ingredients together as you like them
  2. And dig in!
